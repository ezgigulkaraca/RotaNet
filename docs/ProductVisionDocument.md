# RotaNet

### Optimize Routes. Empower Decisions.

---

# Product Vision

## About RotaNet

RotaNet is an AI-powered logistics decision support platform designed to help businesses optimize their transportation operations. Instead of only generating the shortest route, RotaNet analyzes operational data and provides intelligent recommendations that support better business decisions.

The platform combines mathematical optimization, data analytics and artificial intelligence to reduce operational costs, improve fleet utilization and increase overall logistics efficiency.

---

# Vision

To become one of the most trusted AI-powered decision support platforms that enables logistics teams to make faster, smarter and more sustainable operational decisions.

---

# Mission

RotaNet aims to simplify complex logistics planning processes by combining optimization algorithms with artificial intelligence.

Our mission is not only to create optimized routes, but also to explain why a specific solution is recommended, helping decision-makers understand, compare and improve their logistics operations.

---

# Problem Statement

Many logistics operations still rely on manual planning or basic routing software.

These approaches often result in:

- High fuel consumption
- Inefficient vehicle utilization
- Increased operational costs
- Unnecessary carbon emissions
- Time-consuming planning processes
- Decisions based on experience rather than data
- Lack of alternative scenario analysis

Although existing systems can generate routes, they rarely explain why a route is the best choice or provide strategic insights for managers.

---

# Our Solution

RotaNet provides an intelligent decision support system that combines route optimization, operational analytics and artificial intelligence.

The platform helps logistics managers by:

- Optimizing delivery routes
- Increasing vehicle capacity utilization
- Reducing fuel consumption
- Calculating estimated CO₂ emissions
- Comparing different operational scenarios
- Generating AI-powered decision summaries
- Supporting data-driven logistics planning

Rather than simply finding the shortest path, RotaNet helps businesses make better operational decisions.

---

# Target Users

The first version of RotaNet is designed for:

- Small and medium-sized logistics companies
- Distribution and transportation companies
- Manufacturing companies with logistics operations
- Third-party logistics (3PL) providers
- Industrial engineers
- Logistics planners
- Operations managers

# Core Features (MVP)

- Route Optimization
- Vehicle Capacity Optimization

- AI-Powered Decision Explanation

- Improve vehicle utilization
