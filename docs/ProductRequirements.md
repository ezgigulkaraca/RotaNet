# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

RotaNet

---

## Product Overview

RotaNet is an AI-powered logistics decision support platform designed to optimize transportation operations through mathematical optimization, data analytics and artificial intelligence.

The platform enables logistics companies to optimize delivery routes, improve fleet utilization, reduce operational costs and support strategic decision-making with AI-generated insights.

---

## Problem Statement

Many logistics companies still rely on manual planning or traditional routing software that only focuses on finding the shortest path.

These approaches often ignore operational efficiency, vehicle utilization, sustainability and strategic decision support.

RotaNet addresses these challenges by combining optimization algorithms with artificial intelligence.

---

## Target Users

- Logistics Companies
- Fleet Managers
- Supply Chain Managers
- Transportation Coordinators
- Small and Medium-sized Logistics Businesses

---

## Core Features

### Route Optimization

Generate optimized delivery routes using mathematical optimization algorithms.

### Fleet Optimization

Maximize vehicle utilization while minimizing travel distance.

### Interactive Dashboard

Display operational KPIs through a modern dashboard.

### AI Decision Support

Explain why a route is recommended and suggest possible improvements.

### Scenario Analysis

Compare multiple transportation scenarios before execution.

### Sustainability Analysis

Estimate CO₂ emissions and support greener logistics operations.

---

## Functional Requirements

The system shall:

- Import transportation datasets
- Optimize delivery routes
- Calculate total travel distance
- Calculate estimated transportation costs
- Display operational metrics
- Generate AI-supported recommendations
- Visualize optimized routes on an interactive map
